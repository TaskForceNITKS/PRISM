Student Mentorship Program

Why SMP?

The transition from school to college and from home to hostel is hardly an easy one. The pangs of moving away from family for the first time and adjusting in a new place cannot be eliminated but can definitely be made easy. After all, as the popular saying goes, help will always be given to those who require it.

Who is behind this?

The NITK Student Council has come up with a noble initiative to help the freshers with this transition. The Student Mentorship Programme, launched for the first time, this programme aims to acquaint the first years with the different aspects of college life at NITK Surathkal.

What does it contain?

From clubs to sports, academics to fests, the SMP provides a platform for the newbies to explore the different opportunities provided by the college and make the most out of it.
