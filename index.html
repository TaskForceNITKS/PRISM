Student Mentorship Program

The transition from school to college and from home to hostel is hardly an easy one. The pangs of moving away from family for the first time and adjusting in a new place cannot be eliminated but can definitely be made easy. After all, help will always be given to those who require it.

The NITK Student Council has come up with a noble initiative to help the freshers with this transition. The Student Mentorship Programme, launched for the first time, aims to acquaint the first years with the different aspects of college life.

From clubs to sports, academics to fests, the SMP provides a platform for the newbies to explore the different opportunities provided by the college and make the most out of it.

So, go ahead and check out the different tabs for more information and to get to know our college better.
